FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about buying property with us

What documents are required to buy a property?

You'll need identity proof (Aadhar/PAN), address proof, income proof (salary slips/ITR), bank statements, and passport-size photographs. We assist you with the complete documentation process.

Do you help with home loan processing?

Yes! We have tie-ups with leading banks like SBI, HDFC, ICICI, and Axis Bank. We help you get pre-approved loans with competitive interest rates and guide you through the entire process.

Are all your properties legally verified?

Absolutely! Every property we list undergoes thorough legal verification including title check, encumbrance certificate, approved building plans, and all necessary NOCs. Your investment is 100% safe with us.

What is the average property price in Virar?

Property prices in Virar range from ₹25-50 Lakhs for 1 BHK, ₹40-70 Lakhs for 2 BHK, and ₹60 Lakhs to ₹1.2 Cr for 3 BHK apartments. Villas and row houses start from ₹80 Lakhs onwards.

Do you provide after-sale support?

Yes, we provide complete after-sale support including property registration assistance, society transfer, utility connections, and any other help you need even after possession.
